About Healthway

Healthway started as an idea to provide patients easy way to sort and manage the medical reports from multiple clinics, hospitals, doctors and pharmacies. Its difficult to look back a year or month old report to refer to. Healthway resolves this issue by providing an application that can be used to upload and sort reports easily. Healthway also provides platform to approach doctors and request appointments
